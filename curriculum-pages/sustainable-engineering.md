## Week 1 Lesson Outline (MP3)
- Week window: `2026-02-01` to `2026-02-05`
- Lesson 1 (Monday 2026-02-02): Introduction to Sustainability and Environmental Systems
  - Objectives:
    - Students will understand the definition and pillars of sustainability
    - Students will analyze current environmental challenges
    - Students will recognize interconnections in Earth systems
  - NJ Standards: 5.1.8.A, 5.2.8.A
- Lesson 2 (Tuesday 2026-02-03): Climate Change and Global Impact
  - Objectives:
    - Students will understand the science of climate change
    - Students will evaluate evidence and understand climate models
    - Students will recognize global impacts of climate change
  - NJ Standards: 5.1.8.A, 5.2.8.A
- Lesson 3 (Thursday 2026-02-05): Sustainable Development Goals Framework
  - Objectives:
    - Students will understand the 17 UN Sustainable Development Goals
    - Students will identify engineering opportunities within SDGs
    - Students will select a SDG focus area for their unit project
  - NJ Standards: 8.2.2.A.1, 8.2.2.B.1
